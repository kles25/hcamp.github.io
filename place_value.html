PLACE VALUE

Place value is a fundamental concept in mathematics that underpins our entire number system. It is the foundation for understanding how numbers work, how they are represented, and how we perform mathematical operations. Learning place value is essential for students at all levels of math education, from the early years of elementary school to more advanced math concepts.
